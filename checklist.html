‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Deadlines

  • School Enrollment: Must be completed before school year starts (September)
  • Visa Applications: Can take 3-6 months, start early
  • Housing: Vancouver market is competitive, secure early
  • Moving Companies: International moves need 6-8 weeks lead time
  • Medical Records: Can take weeks to obtain from multiple providers

üí° Pro Tips

  • Keep digital copies of all important documents in cloud storage
  • Vancouver has a high cost of living - budget accordingly
  • Canadian banking requires building credit history from scratch
  • BC has different school calendar and curriculum than California
  • Weather is very different - invest in proper rain gear and winter clothes
  • Healthcare is publicly funded but may have wait times for specialists
  • Consider joining Facebook groups for Americans/expats in Vancouver
